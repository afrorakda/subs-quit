# SUBS QUIT | Fridge Combo #113

A minimalist defense tool against forgotten subscriptions. It provides a visual countdown to your next billing date, helping you "quit" before the charge hits.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Focuses entirely on the exit strategy of a subscription.
- **Visual Urgency**: Uses a countdown and color-coded warnings to highlight upcoming and past deadlines.
- **Privacy-First**: No accounts, no tracking. All your service names and dates stay in your browser's local storage.

## Features
- **Smart Countdown**: Shows exactly how many days remain until the next bill.
- **Status Indicators**:
    - **Black (#333)**: Safe for now.
    - **Red (#e74c3c)**: **Next bill is TODAY.** Quit now to avoid charges.
    - **Orange (#f39c12)**: The date has already passed.
- **Quick Entry**: Add service names and billing dates in seconds. Includes a clear button for rapid input.
- **Persistent List**: Your "Exit List" is saved automatically in your browser so you won't lose track.

## Usage
1. Enter the name of the service (e.g., Netflix, Adobe).
2. Select the **Next Billing Date** (the day the charge occurs).
3. Click **ADD TO LIST**.
4. Check the list regularly to see which subscriptions are nearing their "Quit" day.
5. Once you've cancelled a service, tap the **×** to remove it from your list.

## Privacy & Performance
- **Browser Only**: No cloud syncing, no login, no leaks.
- **No Ads**: A clean, focused interface for immediate action.
- **Lightweight**: Optimized to run instantly on low-spec mobile devices and PCs.
